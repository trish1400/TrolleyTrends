How accessible is this site?

It's ok but, as it is a visualiser, there are going to be some limitations.

Most of the information is provided in markup so is accessible.

Colours used for text have a contrast of 3:1 which meets WCAG 2.2 AA as the text is large.

For dynamic charts with unknown series, I've used the Wong and Tol colour palettes, this does mean that they may repeat if you have a lot of series, but I figured that was better for accessibility.

I've added Aria tags and the markup should be decent.

Where possible, i.e. for the stores pie chart, I've repeated the data shown in the chart in the legend.

You can zoom to 400%

All your data is available for download in CSVs.

What's not so accessible?

You can't navigate into the charts using the keyboard.

The animations aren't great for accessibility, but I wanted to tell a story.

Some of the charts on a small screen or at a high zoom are not easy to read.

To be improved

I'm going to explore allowing users to opt out of the animations.
